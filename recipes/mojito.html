Mojito

A minty mojito is the perfect drink for a hot summer's afternoon. We guarantee a single sip of this latin-inspired tipple will transport you to a sun-drenched beach.

DESCRIPTION

Mix together some white rum, sugar, zesty lime and cooling soda water for a winning drink no-one will turn down

INGREDIENTS

  • juice of 1 lime
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • small handful mint leaves, plus extra sprig to serve
  • 60ml white rum
  • soda water, to taste

STEP 1

Muddle the lime juice, sugar and mint leaves in a small jug, crushing the mint as you go - you can use the end of a rolling pin for this. Pour into a tall glass and add a handful of ice.

STEP 2

Pour over the rum, stirring with a long-handled spoon. Top up with soda water, garnish with mint and serve.
